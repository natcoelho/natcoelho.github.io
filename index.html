Hello There

Design with the heart, search for the soul.

I’m Natália Coelho, a Brazilian product designer currently looking for opportunities. As a design lead, I've worked in crossfunctional multidisciplinary teams for the past 10 years delivering product solutions for a range of companies, in a variety of industries. My portfolio includes user interface design, brand & identity design, illustration, and art & creative direction.

I believe that great product experience goes beyond what´s on the screen. It´s about exploring questions of authenticity, purpose and human values in order to craft meaningfull solutions that one can really relate to. I feel like I´m not done until I find the project´s soul. And in order to develop customer experiences with a soul we must link the tools, techniques and frameworks with real people´s needs. So, If you think a brazilian heart would be a good fit for your team, please get in touch.

Better design, better experiences.

Let's guide design teams and make art through these services

Brand Identity

Your brand identity is how people perceive your company, it’s what they say about your business when their friends ask what you do and what they think when they see your name. It starts with a great logo design and it continues with colour and graphics, style guides and brand books. During the past 10 years, I’ve helped bring to life brands that reflect company’s values, voice, products, and target audience.

Illustration

A lot of what I do involves creating striking vector illustrations for a variety of brand products. But I also love the old school, organic touch of producing artworks in pencil, chalk, watercolour, pen and pastel. There’s no need to limit your creativity. If you have an idea that you want realised in any medium, we’ll discover together the suitable technic for your job.

Graphic Design

Having worked in two proeminent companies in the Brazil´s fashion industry, I am highly qualified to offer everything a traditional graphic design agency would and more. I have backgrounds in book, print and magazine design, as well as creating saleable packaging, custom artwork, banners and billboards, and even clothing.

UX/UI Design

With over 7 years experience working from concept to buildout, I'm comfortable within the full spectrum of the product design process. This may look like user research, wireframing, prototyping, high fidelity visual designs, or pair design with engineering teams. Whatever you may need, we can create, design and build together.

Strategic Design

Working together, we'll co-create a design + business strategy that can address your company's core questions and aim to solve key problems with thoughtful and tailored design execution.

Design Workshops

While we can do great work remotely, sometimes nothing beats getting together in person. Through Design Thinking workshops and lectures, conduncing innovation games or coaching and mentorship, I'm available for one-on-one (or one-to-many) in-person sessions.
